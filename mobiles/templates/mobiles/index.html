Textivist - SMS Campaigns & Notifications - Prototype

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Textivist is a tool to send bulk and semi-anonymous text messages. Members of both the general public and your organisation can register their mobiles with Textivist, and join organisations and sign up to updates. Every organisation - and you can have multiple organisations within your account - gets its own phone number. This enables you to allocate costs to campaigns, especially if you have a legal requirement to file accurate expenses.

Textivist (and its suppliers) adheres to privacy regulations that enable users to block messages at any time.

This is currently a prototype, not a fully functional application. Follow the "Try it out!" instructions if you'd like to see how the core functionality works.

Application Structure

Accounts

The top level structure within Textivist. It is broadly assumed that an account represents a single overall organisation, which users would expect to hold accountable. For instance, while most political campaigning happens at a street level, people expect to be able to raise issues such as unwanted messages and have it dealt with throughout the UK.

Organisations

Most application users will be focused on a particular organisation (large campaigning enterprises, such as political parties, have many teams. A user may have broad or narrow interests). This also enables Textivist to provide separate invoices per organisation. It can even (once implemented!) provide a breakdown of costs by topic.

Mobiles

Users register their mobile with Textivist, and then join multiple accounts and organisations. They can self-manage which topics they receive messages about.

Topics and Subscriptions

Every account and organisation might have one or more topics, which mobiles can be subscribed to.

Try it out!

  1. Register your mobile: text "REGISTER" to 01268 833 501
    • You should receive a confirmation text with password.
    • Note that Textivist uses your number including international code e.g. +4471234123456
  2. Set your name: text "SET OWNER Firstname Lastname" to 01268 833 501
  3. Subscribe to a topic: text "ADD ELEX" to 01268 833 501
  4. Schedule a bulk message: Click here, or Actions->Schedule Message:
    • You will be asked to login. Use the phone number and password from your confirmation text.
    • Topic = "Elections In My Area"
    • Pick a time to send, in the format: "2017-03-14 15:00" (we suggest 3-5 minutes in the future)
    • Leave Type and Priority as the defaults of "SMS" and "Normal Messages"
    • Body: Enter any message you like! (Don't forget that SMS messages should be less than 160 characters)
    • Click "Schedule Message" (You will be returned to the home page)
  5. While waiting for your text, check you have been registered here.
  6. Verify that your permissions prevent you from sending bulk messages from your mobile:
    • Text "CAST ELEX This message will be rejected" to 01268 833 501
    • You should receive a "Permission denied." message.
  7. Mute a topic: text "MUTE ELEX" to 01268 833 501
    • Just re-send "ADD ELEX" to re-subscribe
  8. Block all messages from 01268 833501: Text "STOP" to 01268 833 501
    • These commands also work: STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT.
    • Note: need a policy and logic to block *all* phone numbers associated with an account.
    • Potentially, upon receiving a STOP (or just STOPALL?), could check if mobile is registered to other accounts and send a text asking if they'd like them all blocked.
  9. Remove block: Text "START" to 01268 833 501.
    • These commands also work: START, YES, UNSTOP.
    • They will also be treated as registration requests.
